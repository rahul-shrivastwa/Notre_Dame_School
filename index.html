PRINCIPAL’S MESSAGE

“Education is shared commitment between dedicated teachers, motivated students and enthusiastic parents with high expectations.”

This school, the temple of God had been bringing out the persons of character, discipline, academic excellence and greater values from its inception. It has set up high benchmarks of value based holistic education. It has been imparting knowledge and skills that have contributed towards the growth and vision in building global citizens and conscientious humanity.…

“The purpose of education is to make good human beings with skill and expertise. Enlightened human beings can be created by teachers”.
-(Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam)

“Educating the mind without educating the hearts is no education at all”.
-(Aristotle)

Let this year, be an year of many blessings to all of us in the Notre Dame Family and to all who are associated with us.

SR. MARY REMYA S.N.D.
